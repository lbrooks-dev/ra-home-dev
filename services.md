_Services_

#### Three ways to put the method to work.

We offer three things: Training, Certification, and Engagement. Design and operate are not separate practices here. Most firms make you choose — a consulting firm hands you a strategy and leaves, an agency executes work it didn't design. RAOS collapses that line.

Because the method is human-orchestrated and AI-operated, a small author-led team can both design the architecture and run it — the work that once required a large bench. So we don't sell advice and execution as two products. We sell the architecture, and the operation of it.

_Training_

#### Learn to run RAOS.

Training is where most teams and practitioners begin. We bring the operating model to your people, in your context, so RAOS becomes how the work actually gets done — not a binder that sits on a shelf after rollout.

It is more than train-the-trainer. We train practitioners who will deliver the method, and we train in-house revenue teams and agencies who will run it day to day.

**Initial training.** A focused six-hour onboarding for licensed users — the layers, the playbooks, and how to put RAOS to work on your own revenue architecture.

**Recurring training. **Annual training for RAOS licensed subscribers, so your team stays current as the canon and the agents evolve.

**Who it is for**

Practitioners adopting the method, and in-house revenue teams and agencies that want the operating model in the hands of their own people. Training is the step before certification.

_Certification_

#### Become a Certified Revenue Architect™

Certification comes after training. Once you have learned the method, certifying licenses you to practice Revenue Architecture to the standard we set: you take the skill files, the badge, and a method that holds, and you carry it into your own client work with the firm’s support behind you.

It is the credential no agency can manufacture. You are not adopting a framework you found; you are practicing the canon, kept current by the people who wrote it.

**Who it is for**

Trained practitioners, single-shingle consultants, and small consultancies who want a method that scales with them rather than living in their head.

_Engagement_

##### The authors design it — and help you operate it.

This is the work the firm was built for: the authors take direct responsibility for a revenue architecture and help make it run. We work in two modes, and we’ll tell you which one fits in the first conversation.

Engagements are not only for end clients. We also work alongside practitioner teams running RAOS — bringing the authors in on the work where the architecture has to hold.

**Advisory-led. **We architect; you run. We design the revenue architecture, sequence the plays, and hand it to your team with the playbooks and the orchestration to operate it.

**Operated / done-with-you. **We run it with you — agents operating the plays under our orchestration, the authors accountable for the result. You get reach without a buildout.

A limited number of slots, by design. Because Sherwin and John take the work themselves, we are deliberate about which engagements we accept — the ones where the architecture has to hold.
